# Half Cylinder

A cylinder cut in half lengthwise, creating a half-pipe or gutter shape.

<!--  Screenshot of a Half Cylinder primitive on the workspace -->
![20260318 183306 paste 20260318 183306](https://matterhackers.github.io/MatterCAD_Docs/assets/20260318-183306-paste-20260318-183306.jpg)


## Parameters

- **Width** - Size across the flat side (default: 20mm)
- **Depth** - Size from flat side to curved side (default: 20mm)
- **Sides** - Number of segments around the curved surface (default: 20)

## Tips

- Useful for creating channels, gutters, troughs, and rounded edges
- Rotate to use as an arch or tunnel cross-section
- Combine with a [Cube](cube.md) for objects with one rounded side
