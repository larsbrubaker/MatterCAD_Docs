# Cloud Library

The Cloud Library stores your designs online in your MatterHackers account. This lets you access your designs from any computer and keep a backup in the cloud.

![20260323 210141 paste 20260323 210141](https://matterhackers.github.io/MatterCAD_Docs/assets/20260323-210141-paste-20260323-210141.jpg)

## Getting Started

1. Sign in to your MatterHackers account from the application menu
2. The Cloud Library appears in your library browser
3. Upload designs by dragging them into the cloud folder, or save directly to the cloud

## Features

- **Access anywhere** - Your designs are available on any computer where you sign in
- **Cloud backup** - Your work is safely stored online
- **Folder organization** - Create folders to organize your cloud designs just like the local library
- **Sharing** - Share designs with other MatterCAD users. See [Sharing](sharing.md)

## Tips

- Designs are synced automatically when you save to the cloud library
- You need an internet connection to access cloud library content
- Large files may take a moment to upload or download
- Keep important designs in both local and cloud libraries for redundancy
