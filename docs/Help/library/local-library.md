# Local Library

The Local Library stores your 3D designs on your computer. You can organize designs into folders, search for files, and quickly add them to your workspace.

![20260323 210208 paste 20260323 210208](https://matterhackers.github.io/MatterCAD_Docs/assets/20260323-210208-paste-20260323-210208.jpg)


## Browsing the Library

1. Open the **Library** panel from the sidebar or Library tab
2. Navigate through folders to find your designs
3. Double-click a design to open it, or drag it into your workspace to add it to the current scene

## Organizing Your Files

- **Create folders** - Right-click in the library and choose Create Folder to organize your designs
- **Rename items** - Right-click on any item and choose Rename
- **Delete items** - Right-click and choose Delete to remove items
- **Move items** - Drag items between folders to reorganize

## View Options

The library panel supports multiple view modes:

- **List view** - Compact list showing file names
- **Icon view** - Thumbnail previews of your designs
- **Large icon view** - Larger thumbnails for easier browsing

You can sort items by:

- **Name** - Alphabetical order
- **Date Created** - Newest or oldest first
- **Date Modified** - Most recently changed first

## Tips

- Use the search bar at the top of the library to quickly find designs by name
- The library browser shows a preview thumbnail for most file types
- Designs saved in MCX format preserve all parameters and the design tree for full re-editing
