# Viewport Navigation

The 3D viewport is where you view and interact with your designs. MatterCAD uses a trackball camera model that lets you orbit, pan, and zoom freely around your objects.

![20260324 080636 paste 20260324 080636](https://matterhackers.github.io/MatterCAD_Docs/assets/20260324-080636-paste-20260324-080636.jpg)



## Mouse Controls

### Rotate the View

- **Right-click + Drag** - Orbit the camera around your design
- **Ctrl + Left-click + Drag** - Alternative rotation method

### Pan the View

- **Middle-click + Drag** - Slide the view left, right, up, or down
- **Ctrl + Shift + Left-click + Drag** - Alternative pan method

### Zoom In and Out

- **Scroll Wheel** - Scroll up to zoom in, scroll down to zoom out
- **Ctrl + Alt + Left-click + Drag** - Alternative zoom method

## Keyboard Controls

### View Shortcuts

- **W** - Reset the view to fit all objects in the viewport
- **Z** - Zoom to the currently selected object

### Arrow Key Navigation

- **Arrow Keys** - Rotate the view
- **Shift + Arrow Keys** - Pan the view

### Zoom Shortcuts

- **Ctrl + Plus (+)** - Zoom in
- **Ctrl + Minus (-)** - Zoom out

## Tips

- If you lose sight of your model, press **W** to reset the view and bring everything back into frame
- Use **Z** after selecting an object to quickly focus on it
- The scroll wheel zoom is centered on your mouse cursor position, so point at the area you want to zoom into
- You can combine rotation and panning to inspect any angle of your design
