- [2D Paths](2d-paths/index.md) - Flat shapes that can be extruded or revolved into 3D objects
- [Mechanical Parts](mechanical/index.md) - Gears, threads, and other mechanical components

- [Boolean](operations/boolean/index.md) - Combine, subtract, and intersect objects
- [Transform](operations/transform/index.md) - Move, rotate, scale, and mirror
